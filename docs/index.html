What these artifacts are good for

A certificate attests one module version, in one approved-mode configuration, at one moment. Read across the whole corpus it still delivers three things a reviewer can act on: a map of the trusted-computing-base surfaces around each module, a measure of how far its named components have drifted since validation, and a ranked view of where a review should look first. It is best read as a map of what to verify in a deployment, which is what makes it a fast way to aim that verification.
